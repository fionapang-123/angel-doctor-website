# Angel Doctor Website Goal

## 1. Main Goal

Launch Angel Doctor Phase 1 frontend website as a GEO-first, conversion-focused international medical travel platform.

The website should help international patients understand healthcare options in China, trust Angel Doctor’s coordination and medical escort system, submit a lightweight inquiry, and eventually purchase Angel Doctor service packages.

## 2. Project Phase

Current phase:

Phase 1 Frontend Only

Future phase:

Phase 2 Supabase + Stripe + backend operations

## 3. Phase 1 Success Definition

Phase 1 is successful when Angel Doctor has a credible, mobile-friendly, GEO-ready website deployed on Vercel with:

Clear positioning.

Strong homepage.

Lightweight lead capture.

Standardized CTA system.

Treatments pages.

Destinations pages.

Hospital Access page.

Why Angel Doctor page.

Pricing guidance page.

Guides foundation.

Trust and legal footer pages.

JSON-LD schema.

Medical disclaimer language.

Mobile-first layout.

No fake hospitals, doctors, prices, testimonials, ratings, or unsupported claims.

## 4. Strategic Priority

The website should prioritize:

Lead capture.

GEO / SEO visibility.

Trust building.

Standardized medical escort positioning.

Clear medical service boundaries.

Fast Vercel deployment.

Easy Phase 2 expansion.

## 5. Core Brand Positioning

Angel Doctor helps international patients access trusted healthcare in China through AI-assisted hospital matching, transparent coordination, and trained local medical escorts.

Angel Doctor is a healthcare coordination and medical travel support platform.

Angel Doctor is not a hospital, clinic, doctor group, emergency medical provider, or substitute for licensed medical advice.

## 6. Core Hero Message

Efficient Care. Fair Price. Clear Guidance.

Alternative approved version:

Fast Care. Fair Price. Clear Guidance.

## 7. Brand Mindset Slogan

China Healthcare, Guided End to End.

## 8. Core User Benefit

Access China’s top hospitals with a dedicated medical escort by your side.

## 9. Business Goal

Turn website visitors into qualified leads.

Target user path:

Content marketing traffic→ Website visit→ Trust and service understanding→ Get Your Care Plan / Book Local Medical Escort→ Coordinator follow-up→ Service package purchase→ Hospital medical fees paid directly by patient to provider

## 10. Commercial Model

Angel Doctor earns revenue through:

Productized service packages.

Medical escort service fees.

Care coordination service fees.

Translation or medical record preparation support when applicable.

Follow-up coordination or local visit support when applicable.

Hospital medical fees are paid directly by patients to hospitals, clinics, or healthcare providers.

Angel Doctor should not be positioned as collecting or controlling hospital medical fees unless this is separately confirmed.

## 11. Core Conversion Actions

Use only two primary CTA paths in the MVP:

### 11.1 Global CTA

Label:

Get Your Care Plan

Purpose:

For users who want help understanding care options in China.

Route:

/get-care-plan

### 11.2 Medical Escort CTA

Label:

Book Local Medical Escort

Purpose:

For users who need in-hospital or in-clinic support in China.

Route:

/book-local-medical-escort

Do not use “Buy Now”, “Guaranteed Appointment”, “Skip the Queue”, or “Upload Full Medical Case” as primary CTAs.

## 12. Website Must Prove

The website must prove that Angel Doctor is not a loose agent or random interpreter marketplace.

The website should communicate that 600+ medical escorts are supported by:

Training system.

Service standards.

Backend coordination.

Privacy agreements.

Complaint handling.

Emergency escalation mechanism.

Service records.

Coordinator oversight.

Follow-up communication.

The goal is to make medical escort service feel like a standardized product, not a temporary person-finding service.

## 16. Page Role Rules

Use this page logic:

/guides/ = GEO / SEO content discovery.

/treatments/ = service conversion and patient inquiry.

/destinations/ = city-based conversion and GEO visibility.

/hospital-access = hospital network trust and structured GEO support.

Do not turn Hospital Access into a raw hospital directory.

## 22. Pricing Goal

Pricing should explain cost components without overpromising.

Cost components:

Angel Doctor coordination fees.

Medical escort fees.

Travel, hotel, and local transportation costs.

Do not claim:

Guaranteed lowest price.

Exact price before consultation.

No hidden costs guaranteed.

Guaranteed appointment.

Guaranteed doctor access.

Guaranteed outcome.

## 25. Mobile Goal

The website must be mobile-first.

Mobile rules:

Same URL and same content as desktop.

No separate mobile pages.

Single-column layout on mobile.

Primary CTA visible above the fold.

Sticky bottom CTA on key conversion pages.

FAQ accordion on mobile.

Avoid long paragraphs.

Test at 375px, 390px, 768px, 1024px, and desktop width.
