Homepage structure preview: less text, less AI

These are preview-only layout directions. The goal is to keep trust and GEO value, but make the page feel more human: fewer explanations, stronger choices, clearer next steps.

Cut paragraphsUse one short idea per section.
Lead with choicesLet users pick treatment, city, or support need.
Keep proof compactUse trust facts once, not repeatedly.
Move detail downUse FAQs and guides for long explanations.
